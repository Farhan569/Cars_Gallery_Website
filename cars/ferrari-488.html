About the Ferrari 488

The Ferrari 488 represents the perfect blend of racing technology and road-going comfort. It features advanced aerodynamics, exceptional handling, and the unmistakable Ferrari V8 engine sound.

With its twin-turbo V8 engine, the 488 delivers incredible performance with improved efficiency. The advanced aerodynamics and precise handling make it both track-capable and comfortable for road use, embodying Ferrari's racing heritage.
